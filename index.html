Mike Patton

The Man of A Thousand Voices

A Chronology of one of musics most diverse artists

  • Born Januray 27, 1968 in Eureka California
  • 1985 Mike Forms Avante Garde Experi-metal band Mr Bungle
  • 1988 Patton drops out of University to join Faith No More
  • 1989 Faith No More releases "The Real Thing", their first albume with Mike Patton and their third altogether.
  • 1990 Faith No More sit comfortably in Billboards top 10 with a video for their hit "Epic". Featuring Mike in a Mr. Bungle T-Shirt & a tutu. (some marketing right?)
  • Mr. Bungle Releases their first studio album in 1990 at the peak of Faith No More's meteoric Success
  • Faith No More Releases 3 more albums before disbanding in 1998
  • 1999 Mike Patton forms record label Ipecac Recordings with his new Progressive Rock band Tomahawk being its first release.
  • Tomahawk released 3 albums between 2000 & 2007
  • 1999 Mr. Bungle releases their first and only Makor Label Record "California" under Warner Bros (talk about full circle). But they stopped touring between 2000 & 2004 innevitably breaking up
  • In 2002 Patton joins Math Core band The Dillinger Escape Plan to record vocals after their original singer left. Providing visceral screams, infectious melodies and a sense of chaos that could only be matched by a nightful of lightning in an uncontrolled forest fire.
  • 2004 Patton assits Scandonavian artist Bjork with her album "Medulla"
  • 2005 Patton collaborates with hip-hop trio The X-Ecutioners to to release the album "General Patton vs The X-Ectuioners"
  • 2006 Mike forms and releases self titled album "Peeping Tom"
  • 2007 Mike takes on the challenge of the italian language by performing a collection of italian pop songs from the 50s and 50s under the name "Mondo Cane"
  • Patton continues to stretch his vocal chops in 2007 with VA work in Will Smith film "I am Legend" as the infected. He joined the gaming industry by voicing The Darkness in the game titled "The Darkness" & voiced the Anger Core in the hit game "Portal"
  • 2008 Mike Joins TV On The Radio frontman Tunde Adimpe & rapper Doseone on a collaborative effort called "Nevermen".
  • 2008 also sees Mike Join the cast of Metalocalypse to play Rikki Kixx
  • 2009 Faith No More gets back together for "The Second Coming tour" and several others.
  • 2009 Patton writes his first score for Hollywood blockbuster "Crank"
  • MIke is hired to write the film score for Ryan Gosling film "The Place Beyond the Pines"
  • 2015 Faith No More releases first album in 16 years titled "Sol Invictus"
  • 2016 Nevermen releases first album under Ippecac Records after an 8 year writing period.
  • 2017 Mike writes music for Stephen king based horror film "1922"
  • 2017 Mike Patton Joins hardcore band "Dead Cross" at 49 screaming just as he did at 19
